Projects, Competitions and Events I have participated in:

Re->Cycle

  • In 2021, I and my team decided to participate in 'HackTuesGG' - a 3-day-long hackaton, organised by TUES of TU - Sofia, Bulgaria.
  • Main topic - 'Go Green'.
  • We came up with the game 'Re->Cycle' whose main goal is to collect recycable rubbish around the map and throw it away in the correct bin.
  • Aim - to create an entertainable software that will encourage the younger generations to recycle properly.
  • My role - the leader of the team, supervising the whole process of production and providing help if needed.

Teacher Tower

  • In 2021-2022, I participated in 'SciFest@2021-2022' with my team.
  • We created an educational software - the game 'Teacher Tower'.
  • Main goal - to help the adventurer Yuki escape the King Brick's prison for travellers.
  • They have to solve various puzzles from different school subjects.
    • Along with SciFest, we joined 'Student Enterprise Competition', so with 'Teacher Tower' we won multiple awards in different competitions.
